9 /**@brief A class inheriting from a TF1 that assists in the fitting of a
10  * reaction rate to the JINA REACLIB format.
11  * @author Karl Smith
12  *
13  * The REACLIB format is a set of seven parameters that provide a functional
14  * form for a reaction rate. The expression is designed to provide terms that
15  * will fit both non-resonant, charge particle and neutron induced, as well as
16  * narrow resonances. This class will take physical parameters (reactant
17  * charges, reduced mass, S(0), number of resonances, resonance energies,
18  * and resonance strengths) and make educated guess about initial values. After
19  * the fit is performed the resulting physical parameters can be extracted.
20  */
